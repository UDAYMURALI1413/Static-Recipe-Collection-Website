Vegetable Samosa

Description

Crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas, deep-fried until golden. A classic Indian snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled, diced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Ready-made samosa pastry sheets or dough
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and ginger.
  2. Step 2: Add potatoes, peas, spices, and salt. Mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Let mixture cool. Fill samosa pastry sheets with mixture, fold and seal edges.
  4. Step 4: Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Step 5: Serve with chutney.

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 8 samosas

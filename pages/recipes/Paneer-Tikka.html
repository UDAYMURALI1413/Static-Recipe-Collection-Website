Paneer Tikka

Description

Chunks of paneer marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in a tandoor or oven.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Combine all marinade ingredients. Add paneer cubes and coat well. Marinate 30 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Thread onto skewers with bell peppers and onions if desired.
  3. Step 3: Grill or bake in a hot oven (220°C/430°F) for 15 minutes, or until lightly charred.
  4. Step 4: Serve with mint chutney.

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 2-3

Gulab Jamun

Description

Soft milk-based balls, deep-fried and soaked in fragrant sugar syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup khoya or milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • A pinch of baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons milk (as needed for dough)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Mix khoya, flour, baking soda, and enough milk to form a soft dough.
  2. Step 2: Shape into small balls.
  3. Step 3: Heat sugar and water. Add cardamom powder to make syrup.
  4. Step 4: Deep fry balls on low heat until golden.
  5. Step 5: Soak hot gulab jamuns in warm syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 12 pieces

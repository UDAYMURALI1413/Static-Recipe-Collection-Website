Chana Masala

Description

Hearty, tangy curry cooked with chickpeas, tomatoes, and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Heat oil, saut√© onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Step 2: Add tomatoes and all spices, cook until oil separates.
  3. Step 3: Add chickpeas, mix well, cook for 10 minutes, add water for gravy.
  4. Step 4: Simmer on low for 10 more minutes.
  5. Step 5: Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with rice or roti.

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

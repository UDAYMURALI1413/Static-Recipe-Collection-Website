Simple Lemon Vinaigrette

Description

A bright and zesty homemade salad dressing that comes together in minutes. Perfect for green salads or drizzling over grilled vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (120ml) extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) fresh lemon juice (about 1 large lemon)
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • Optional: 1/2 teaspoon honey or maple syrup for a touch of sweetness
<

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Juice the lemon into a small bowl or jar.
  2. Step 2: Add the Dijon mustard, salt, pepper, and optional garlic/sweetener to the lemon juice. Whisk to combine.
  3. Step 3: While whisking continuously, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the dressing emulsifies (thickens slightly and combines).
  4. Step 4: Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary (more salt, pepper, or lemon juice).
  5. Step 5: Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week. Whisk again before serving as it may separate.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Servings: 3-4 cups

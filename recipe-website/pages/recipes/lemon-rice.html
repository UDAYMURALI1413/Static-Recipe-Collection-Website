Lemon Rice

Description

A vibrant and flavorful South Indian rice dish, Lemon Rice is quick to prepare and makes for a fantastic light meal or lunchbox option.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover and cooled)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • A few curry leaves
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Juice of 1-2 lemons (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Ensure rice is cooled and separate the grains with a fork if clumped.
  2. Step 2: Heat oil in a large pan or kadai. Add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn light golden brown.
  3. step 3: Add peanuts and sauté until roasted and crunchy.
  4. Step 4: Add green chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
  5. Step 5: Reduce heat to low. Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  6. Step 6: Add the cooked rice to the pan. Gently mix everything together, ensuring the spices are evenly distributed. Be careful not to mash the rice.
  7. Step 7: Remove from heat. Add lemon juice and salt to taste. Mix well.
  8. Step 8: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 2-3

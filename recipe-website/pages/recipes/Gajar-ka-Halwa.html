Gajar ka Halwa

Description

A traditional carrot-based sweet treat made with carrots, milk, ghee, and nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrots, grated
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 10-12 cashews and almonds, chopped
  • 10-12 raisins
  • 1/2 cup khoya (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a heavy pan, cook grated carrots and milk until milk is absorbed.
  2. Step 2: Add khoya (if using), ghee, sugar, and cardamom powder. Cook, stirring, until mixture thickens.
  3. Step 3: Stir in nuts and raisins. Cook until glossy and fragrant.
  4. Step 4: Serve warm.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Servings: 4

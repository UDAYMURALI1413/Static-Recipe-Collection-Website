Butter Chicken

Description

Creamy tomato-based curry with marinated chicken, a North Indian restaurant favorite.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Step 2: Grill or cook chicken pieces. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: In a pan, heat butter, add tomato puree, simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Add cooked chicken, cream, sugar, and simmer another 10 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Serve hot with naan or rice.

Prep Time: 20 minutes + marination

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4
